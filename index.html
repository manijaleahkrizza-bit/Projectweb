Must-See Gems

What's in Bohol?

From iconic geological marvels to wildlife encounters — experience the heart of the Visayas.

Chocolate Hills

Over 1,200 symmetrical hills that turn chocolate-brown in summer — a UNESCO world heritage contender and the Philippines' national geological monument.

Carmen, Bohol

Philippine Tarsier

One of the world's smallest primates, with enormous eyes. Visit the conservation sanctuary and witness these gentle, nocturnal creatures up close.

Corella, Bohol

Loboc River Cruise

Floating restaurant adventure with lush jungle backdrop, live cultural shows, and a refreshing dip at Busay Falls.

Loboc, Bohol

Alona Beach

Powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, world-class diving spots & vibrant nightlife — the heart of island relaxation.

Panglao Island

Blood Compact Shrine

Historic Sandugo between Spanish explorer Legazpi and Datu Sikatuna — symbolizes friendship and Filipino heritage.

Tagbilaran City

Hinagdanan Cave

Stunning underground cavern with a natural lagoon, stunning stalactites, and ethereal lighting for nature lovers.

Dauis, Panglao
